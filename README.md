# Video Quest

## The Video Quest is designed to enhance productivity and learning by distilling lengthy videos into concise, digestible summaries. Leveraging advanced Al algorithms, this app identifies the most relevant insights within videos, and provides a comprehensive overview.

## Video Quest serves a twofold purpose:
### - Firstly, it offers a summarization feature that distills the essence of You Tube videos, providing users with concise overviews.
### - Secondly, it delivers QnA and Topic Insights drawn from a pre-indexed video store, such as SharePoint, enabling users to quickly access and engage with the content's key points.
